Staff Rules

General Conduct

SPaG (Spelling, Grammar and Punctuation) is required while on-duty, if you choose to disregard this rule you will be striked.

  1. You are not allowed to use off-duty commands unless you got perms from @Executive Ownership+, if you use off-duty commands you will also be striked.
  2. You are allowed to kick/ban off-duty if there is no staff online.
  3. You are not allowed to roleplay on-duty, you will receive a warning if you are caught roleplaying on-duty.
  4. The command :h are restricted to Admin+ and the command :m are restricted to Director+.
  5. You must always remain professional and use SPaG in public chats.
  6. Everyone are required to use their uniform or the "Main Staff Uniform", if your team doesn't have a uniform please use the team under your teams uniform.
  7. Your name should take use of this guide: <Rank> | <Your name> | <Your timezone (Optional)>.

Training Guide

Before the Training

The following requirements must be met before beginning the training process:

  • Trainees must use SPaG (Spelling, Punctuation, and Grammar) at all times. If you have to remind them 3+ times, they must be disqualified.
  • Trainees must join the Sheriff team, put on the "Staff trainee" uniform, and sit in the waiting room until you arrive.

Training Rules Agreement

Trainees must agree to these rules before proceeding:

  • You are not allowed to use the :m or :h command for any reasons.
  • You are not allowed to ping OT in our comms server, please DM them instead.
  • You must know the ingame rules while moderating, look at the punishment guide for more information.
  • You are not allowed to use commands off-duty, this is a fireable offense.

If trainees are found not following any of these rules, they will be punished. If they have no questions, you may proceed with the training.

Knowledge Assessment

Trainees have a maximum of 20 minutes to answer the following questions:

  1. What is RDM?
  2. What is Shift Grinding?
  3. Are you able to use your commands while off-duty?
  4. When you see an exploiter ingame, what would you do?
  5. What is LTAP?
  6. What is Staff Evasion?
  7. What is Cuff rushing?
  8. Are you allowed to use :h?
  9. What is tool abuse?
  10. What is the banned roleplays in our server? Hint: SST
  11. Are you allowed to be roleplaying while on-duty?

Assessment Results

If they pass: "Congratulations, you have passed the training part of your training!"

If they fail: "I'm sorry but you have failed the training part, don't worry you have 1 attempt left to try again."

Ride Along (R/A)

The final phase of training involves a practical assessment:

  • High ranking team+ are allowed to host R/As.
  • The trainee is required to do 15 mod calls (HR teleports them around).
  • The R/A must last at least 1 hour.

Evaluation Criteria

The trainee is evaluated on the following criteria:

Criteria Points Range
SPaG 1 - 3 points
Handling mod calls 1 - 4 points
Professionalism 1 - 3 points

Grading

Passing grade: 7 - 10 points

Failing grade: 1 - 6 points

If trainees fail, they have 1 attempt to redo the R/A.
